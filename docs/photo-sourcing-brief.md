# Vyrek photo sourcing brief

**Purpose:** a commission-ready spec you (or a photographer / stock-image agency / casting director) can act on to lift Vyrek's imagery from "decent Pexels stock" to "owned, race-authentic visual identity".

**Reading order:**

1. §A is the **top-priority shoot** — solves the diversity gaps + the brand-defining slots in one half-day. £1,500-3,000 budget.
2. §B is the **station shoot** — the 9 Hyrox stations photographed authentically, half-day, £1,500-3,000.
3. §C is the **stock-library shopping list** — Adobe Stock / Pexels-paid searches for slots that can be solved with licensed stock (~£500-1500 total).
4. §D is the **member testimonial gather** — free, just takes outreach time.
5. §E is **event press photography** — for Part 3 Results hub.

Each priority block lists: slot it solves, current §1.2 verdict, required subject, required composition, required mood, search terms (for stock) or shot list (for commission).

---

## §A — Coach + Brand half-day shoot (HIGHEST PRIORITY)

**Goal:** real, owned imagery of James Wright + a diverse cast of 3-5 athletes at a UK Hyrox-affiliated gym. Replaces every "Pexels coach" + "Pexels portrait" + "Pexels testimonial" slot site-wide.

### Cast (must-have)

1. **James Wright** — founding coach. The Vyrek face.
2. **One female athlete, 35-45, white or Black** — represents the Masters demo currently absent from imagery.
3. **One non-lean first-time athlete, any gender** — represents the First Race programme honestly (current imagery sets a fitness prerequisite that the programme itself doesn't).
4. **One Black or South Asian athlete, 25-35** — closes the ethnicity gap; 4/25 identifiable subjects are non-white today.
5. **Optional: one Adaptive athlete** — if local Hyrox community can introduce one, the visual signal is huge for a £0 incremental cost.

### Venue

- Sweat It (London), ARC (Manchester), Box Hill (Bristol), or any UK gym with: floor signage, sleds, wall ball targets, real Hyrox standard kit. Avoid F45 / Body Pump-branded spaces.

### Shot list (~120 frames)

**A1. James Wright portrait pack (~25 frames)**
- Tight portrait, neutral expression, eye contact — for Coach hub tile, About page, blog author photo (`v2-coach-james-wright`)
- Half-body holding kettlebell — variant for editorial
- Action: demonstrating a sled push — for hero backdrops
- Action: explaining technique to one of the cast athletes — for About hero + Partners hero
- Candid laughing with cast — for testimonial / community surfaces

**A2. Cast athletes — programme cards (~40 frames)**
- *First Race* hero: non-lean first-timer pushing a sled for the first time, slight grimace, controlled
- *Sub-90* hero: athlete mid-race-pace on a station, race-bib visible if possible
- *Doubles* hero: two cast members in mid-handoff at a station — emphasises coordination, not solo work
- *Pro* hero: athlete post-effort, hands on knees, breath visible if cold

**A3. Testimonial portrait pack (~30 frames)**
- One portrait + one action shot per cast member, signed consent forms — replaces every "Pre-launch · illustrative" testimonial
- ASA/CAP code 3.7 compliance: get signed consent forms before any portrait shoot if the image will be used with a quote

**A4. Bento + lifestyle (~25 frames)**
- Phone-in-hand with the Vyrek app open (mock-up) — replaces `bento-plan`
- Watch metrics check post-session — replaces `bento-progress`
- Wide gym-floor scene — replaces `bento-coaches` and the various reused gym backdrops

### Composition guidelines
- All hero plates landscape ≥1920w with text-headroom on the left third
- All portraits 4:5 OR 5:4 (works in any tile crop)
- Editorial palette: cool / neutral, NOT warm — current Pexels coach has the right register; h1/h2 are warmer and should not become the dominant style

### Deliverables
- All RAW + edited JPEG + WebP exports
- Per-image alt text drafted at delivery
- Signed model releases for every visible subject
- Filename convention: `vyrek-{role}-{subject-initials}-{shot-number}.jpg`

### Budget tier
£1,500-3,000 half-day shoot (London / Manchester / Bristol rates). Half goes to photographer, half to cast + venue hire if not free.

£1,500-3,000 same pricing as §A.

## §D — Member testimonial gather (free, ~30 days)

**Goal:** replace the 3 "Pre-launch · illustrative" testimonials with real consented Vyrek graduates + their photo.

### Process

1. Email existing First Race / Sub-90 / Doubles graduates (Supabase `customers` table, filter by programme + completed-quiz timestamp older than 12 weeks)
2. Offer: a free month of Vyrek + a t-shirt + (if they want) feature on the testimonials carousel
3. Required from them: a 30-50 word quote about the programme, a finish-line photo OR a gym-session photo, signed photo + quote release
4. Filter for quality: 3 quotes with concrete numbers ("finished 92 minutes when I'd planned for 105") + 3 photos that fit the testimonial card (5:4 portrait or landscape with face dominant)

### Replaces
- Cluster 3 entirely
- Drops the `illustrative: true` flag in `lib/testimonials.ts`

### Cost
£0 (1-2 t-shirts), ~5 hrs of outreach time.

## §E — Event press photography for Part 3 Results hub

**Goal:** real Hyrox race-day photography for the Results recap pages, venue heroes, and athlete profile thumbnails.

### Sources to investigate

1. **Hyrox official press releases** — sometimes downloadable with attribution from `hyrox.com/press`. Check licensing terms.
2. **Tom Greener / James Appleton / other UK fitness photographers** who regularly shoot Hyrox events — DM for licensing rates per image (~£25-50 typical for editorial use)
3. **Athletes' own social** — for athlete profile thumbnails, ask for permission to use their public race photo
4. **SAP / mika Timing** — they publish event photos alongside results, terms vary

### Cost
£300-1000 depending on how many events / how many images per event.
