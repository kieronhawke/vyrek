The 2026 UK Hyrox calendar is the cleanest it's been since the sport landed in Britain. Four major race weekends across the year, distributed roughly quarterly, all in major-city arena venues with strong transport links. If you're planning your year, this is the page to bookmark.

## The 2026 UK calendar at a glance

**March. Hyrox London at ExCeL**

The flagship event. ExCeL hosts the largest UK Hyrox weekend by athlete count (typically 4,000-6,000 across the two days). DLR drops you at Custom House station, two minutes from the venue entrance. Full venue logistics on the [Hyrox London event page](/hyrox/events/london-excel-march-2026).

**April. Hyrox Manchester at Manchester Central**

The Northern flagship. Smaller field (2,500-3,500) but identical race standards. Five minutes from Piccadilly station, walking distance from most city-centre hotels. Strong local community of athletes from Manchester, Leeds, Liverpool, and the surrounding M62 corridor. [Manchester event details](/hyrox/events/manchester-central-april-2026).

**October. Hyrox Birmingham at the NEC**

The Midlands flagship. Birmingham International rail station is a 5-minute walk from the NEC; direct trains from London Euston take about 75 minutes. Huge on-site parking and hotels (Hilton Birmingham Metropole, Crowne Plaza NEC) literally next door. [Birmingham event details](/hyrox/events/birmingham-nec-october-2026).

**November. Hyrox Glasgow at the OVO Hydro**

The Scottish flagship. The OVO Hydro is a 12,500-capacity arena right on the Clyde, 10 minutes from Glasgow Central station. Easy from Edinburgh (50 min by train) and the rest of central Scotland. [Glasgow event details](/hyrox/events/glasgow-ovo-november-2026).

## Which one should you pick?

**For your first Hyrox:** Manchester or Birmingham. Smaller fields, less queue pressure, easier to find your bearings.

**For the bucket-list experience:** ExCeL London. Largest event, biggest atmosphere, most divisions running.

**For competitive racing:** any of them, race standards are identical. The Pro division at ExCeL tends to attract the deepest field because it's the biggest UK qualifier.

**For accessibility:** depends on where you live. The two-hour drive rule is real, most first-time racers underperform when they've travelled more than two hours that morning. Pick the closest venue you can get to with one travel day before.

## What happens on race weekend

All UK venues run a two-day weekend. Friday is bag-drop and athlete check-in (optional but recommended). Saturday and Sunday run waves throughout the day, most age-group athletes race Saturday; Sunday tends to be Doubles, Pro, and Masters.

Wave times are released about 4 weeks before each event. You'll get an email from Hyrox with your specific time slot. Plan to arrive 90 minutes before your wave for the [full warm-up protocol](/blog/hyrox-race-day-warm-up).

## Travel + accommodation

Each event page has venue-specific transport notes and recommended hotels. The general rules:

- **Book the hotel as soon as you register.** Race-week rooms sell out 3-4 months ahead at London and Birmingham.
- **Walking distance beats cheap-and-distant.** A £40 cheaper room that's a 20-minute taxi ride away is worse than the £40 room next door.
- **Arrive the day before if you're racing.** Don't drive 3 hours the morning of.
- **Train in the destination city if you can the day before.** Find a local PureGym or commercial gym, do a 20-minute mobility flow, build familiarity with the city.

## What's NOT in the 2026 UK calendar

Glasgow is the only Scottish race. Belfast doesn't yet host a Hyrox event. Northern Irish athletes typically travel to Manchester or Liverpool to fly out for Birmingham/London. Cardiff and Bristol haven't had recurring fixtures despite strong local communities.

This will likely change. Hyrox is expanding fast in the UK and additional cities are reportedly under negotiation. We'll update this page as fixtures get announced.

## How to use this for your training year

Pick one A-race. That's the race you taper for, peak for, and emotionally invest in. Pick a B-race 8-12 weeks later if you want a year-round goal, your B-race fitness is the bonus from the A-race build.

Most UK athletes who race well across the year follow this pattern: spring A-race (London or Manchester), autumn A-race (Birmingham or Glasgow). The summer between is base-building.
